Refund Policy

Digital Marketing Services and Courses

Thank you for choosing our digital marketing services and courses. We strive to provide the highest quality service and educational content to help you achieve your marketing goals. To ensure clarity and transparency, please review our refund policy below.

Non-Refundable Payment Policy

  1. Services and Course Fees:
    All payments made for our digital marketing services and digital marketing courses are non-refundable. Once a payment is made, it cannot be returned under any circumstances.
  2. Commitment to Quality:
    We are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and valuable course content. If you have any concerns or issues, please contact our support team. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring you have a positive experience.
  3. Course Access:
    Upon payment, you will receive immediate access to the purchased digital marketing course materials. Due to the nature of digital content, we cannot offer refunds once access has been granted.
  4. Service Delivery:
    For digital marketing services, our team will begin work immediately upon receipt of payment. Given the customized nature of these services, refunds are not available once work has commenced.
  5. Customer Support:
    If you encounter any problems or have questions about our services or courses, please reach out to our support team. We are here to help and ensure that you get the most out of our offerings.
  6. Cancellation Policy:
    While payments are non-refundable, you can cancel future recurring services at any time. Please notify us in writing if you wish to discontinue a recurring service.
  7. Dispute Resolution:
    In the event of any dispute, we encourage you to contact our customer support team. We aim to resolve any issues amicably and to your satisfaction.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. We value your business and are committed to providing you with the best possible digital marketing services and educational resources.

For any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at contact@anaamtiwary.com
